SANGANAN PRAYOG
NIT AGARTALA

Through this club, we aim to create an engaging environment where scientific thought and logical reasoning is encouraged to tackle both previously solved and unsolved questions regarding the universe and the laws of physics

We focus on gathering enthusiasts and facilitating knowledge sharing across all groups of students and to capture the interest of all the students in the mysteries of the universe. We also promote learning from the experts of the research field and the people in the industry

Current Projects

Satellite Development

Study and demonstrate orientational dynamics and control of a satellite

  • ● Working on a miniature working model of a satellite
  • ● Exploring orientational dynamics with attitude description and kinematics
  • ● Study control mechanisms and different sensors used in satellites



OUR HISTORY

Sanganan Prayog, Space, Technology and Computation was founded in 2019
by Sri Ganesh
and based on the idea of developing young minds towards space.

WHAT DO WE DO?

Sky observations

Both in-campus and short excursions
for observation of the night sky,
guided by either members from the club
or professional guides to learn
to observe, identify and deduce the
properties of objects visible in the
night sky through a telescope

Guest Lectures and Workshops:

Lectures and workshops on several
topics in Space, Astronomy and
Mathematics by faculty from colleges
and research institutes from
across India and abroad

Discussions:

Open discussions on recent
and/or important topics in Space,
and Astronomy between
members of the club and guests,
may or may not be aided by a professor


Competitive Events:

Prepare for and participate in
inter-college and inter-institution
events in the fields of Space,
Astronomy and Design


Miscellaneous:

Includes competitive
quizzes, treasure hunts,
research projects, comic design
and more
